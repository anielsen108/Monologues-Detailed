# Marigold Optimism

_Bouncy encouragement, sunrise pep, and bright civic cheer._

Use for rallying daylight marches, pep talks, or domestic pep when morale sags.

## Resonator and control targets

- **Larynx height:** Keep the larynx slightly high for sunshine sparkle.
- **Epilaryngeal (AES) “twang”:** Engage a lively AES ping so hope cuts through noise.
- **Velum (nasality):** Raise the velum fully for ringing, clarion vowels.
- **Pharynx width:** Maintain a medium width that can widen into celebratory beams.
- **Tongue root:** Keep the tongue root forward for nimble, upbeat articulation.
- **Jaw aperture:** Use a lively jaw that bounces open on each lift.
- **Lip shape:** Alternate quick spreads for smiles and rounded moments for reassurance.
- **False vocal folds:** Retract to keep the tone crisp and joyous.
- **Vocal-fold adduction:** Use medium adduction with buoyant onsets.
- **Fold thickness (CT↔TA):** Favor medium-thin folds that can flicker like sunlight.
- **Breath pressure/flow:** Deliver rhythmic bursts of airflow, like pumping fists in the air.
